\# AcePredict Branding Guide



\## Brand Personality



AcePredict should feel:



\* Intelligent

\* Analytical

\* Modern

\* Competitive

\* Premium



It should feel closer to a sports analytics platform than a betting website.



\---



\## Design Inspiration



Primary:



\* ATP Finals

\* Formula1.com

\* F1 Kids



Secondary:



\* Roland Garros

\* Flashscore

\## Mascot System



Use tennis-themed mascots.



Examples:



\* Tennis Fox

\* Tennis Panda

\* Tennis Tiger

\* Tennis Rabbit

\* Tennis Owl

\* Tennis Penguin

\* Tennis Bear

\* Tennis Cat

\* Tennis Koala

\* Tennis Corgi



Requirements:



\* Transparent background

\* Consistent art style

\* Professional

\* Cute but not childish

\* Suitable for a sports analytics platform



\---



\## User Experience Goals



Users should feel:



\* Informed

\* Engaged

\* Curious

\* Competitive



The platform should encourage exploration of player statistics and match predictions.
