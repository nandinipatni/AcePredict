\# AcePredict Frontend Master Specification



\## Product



AcePredict



AI-powered tennis analytics and match prediction platform.



The product should feel like a premium sports analytics startup rather than a college project.



Inspiration:



\* ATP Finals

\* Wimbledon

\* Formula 1

\* F1 Kids

\* Modern AI startups



\---



\## Design Language



\### Theme



Premium sports analytics.



\### Mood



Elegant.



Dynamic.



Modern.



Memorable.



Data-driven.



\### Avoid



Generic dashboards.



Bootstrap appearance.



Template websites.



Casino aesthetics.



Betting aesthetics.
