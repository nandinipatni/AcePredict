\# AcePredict V1 Product Specification



\## Vision



AcePredict is a tennis analytics platform that predicts ATP match outcomes and provides player insights, comparisons, and analytics.



The platform is designed for:



\* Hardcore tennis fans

\* Tennis analysts

\* Fantasy tennis users

\* Recruiters evaluating machine learning projects



The goal is to feel like a professional sports analytics platform rather than a college machine learning assignment.



\---



\## Core Features



\### Match Predictor



Users select:



\* Player A

\* Player B

\* Surface



AcePredict returns:



\* Player A win probability

\* Player B win probability

\* Confidence level



\---



\### Player Profiles



Each player profile displays:



\* ATP Rank

\* ATP Ranking Points

\* Age



Future versions may include:



\* Nationality

\* Player image

\* Surface performance



\---



\### Head-to-Head



Users can compare two players.



Display:



\* Total matches played

\* Wins by Player A

\* Wins by Player B



\---



\### Recent Form



Display:



\* Last 5 matches



Visual format:



W W L W W



or



🟢 🟢 🔴 🟢 🟢



\---



\### Player Comparison



Side-by-side comparison:



\* Rank

\* Points

\* Age

\* Predicted win probability
